GENERAL BACKGROUND

Welcome to Truth Primary School, Ngetta - Lira City East At Truth Primary School, we believe that every child is a seed of greatness. Founded with the mission to provide quality education to the community of Ngetta
and beyond, we have nurtured generations of learners to achieve academic excellence, strong character, and leadership potential. Our school offers a warm, inclusive, and stimulating environment where students can discover and develop their talents under the guidance of skilled and
caring teachers. We are dedicated to laying a strong foundation for lifelong learning. Our Vision To be a leading institution providing holistic education that empowers learners to become responsible citizens and future leaders. Our Mission To deliver high-quality education through innovative teaching methods, moral guidance, and active community involvement. Core Values Integrity:We uphold honesty and transparency in all we do. Excellence:We strive for the highest standards in academics and character. Respect:We value each individual and their unique contributions. Discipline: We promote self-control, responsibility, and hard work. Teamwork: We encourage collaboration among students, staff, and parents. Academics

Truth Primary School offers a robust curriculum approved by the Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB), focusing on:


    Literacy and Language Development
    Mathematics
    Science and Technology
    Social Studies
    Religious Education
    Creative Arts (Music, Dance, Drama, and Fine Art)
    Physical Education
We integrate ICT and modern learning techniques to prepare our students for the demands of the 21st century.
