Truth Primary School, located in Lira, Uganda, has demonstrated commendable academic performance in recent years. According to the 2020 Primary Leaving Examinations (PLE), the school was ranked 263rd among the top 300 primary schools in Uganda . In the 2022 PLE results, Truth Primary School maintained its position at263rd, with 25 candidates sitting for the exams and 10 achieving Division One grades .

This consistent performance indicates a strong academic foundation and effective teaching strategies at Truth Primary School. In a national context where learning outcomes have been a concern highlighted by reports indicating that 64% of Ugandan children in
school are not achieving expected learning outcomes.Truth Primary School's achievements are particularly noteworthy.

For more detailed information on the school's programs, teaching methodologies, or recent initiatives, it would be beneficial to contact the school's administration directly or consult the Lira District Education Office.
